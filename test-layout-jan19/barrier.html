Summary of the problem

Argentina has banned imports of poultry products from the EU (including the UK) due to Spongiform encephalopathy (BSE) since 2001. Several Members States have applied for approval to export poultry to Argentina first group (DK, NL, IE and FR) as of 2006 and second group (AT, BE, ES, DE, HU, IT and SE).
